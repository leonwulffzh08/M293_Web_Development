Welcome to my Yamaha Webshop School Project

Yamaha is one of the world's best-known motorbike brands and has stood for innovation, performance and passion on two wheels for decades. The models range from manoeuvrable city runabouts and sporty naked bikes to uncompromising super sports bikes that are at home on racetracks. Yamaha is particularly appreciated for its high-quality workmanship, striking design and precise handling. Whether beginner or experienced biker - Yamaha offers the right motorbike for almost every riding style. Anyone who values technology, performance and riding enjoyment will find it hard to ignore this brand. In our shop, we present a selection of popular Yamaha models and give you the opportunity to find out more about their strengths and areas of use.

Nakedbike

Yamaha naked bikes combine purist design with impressive performance. Without panelling and with visible technology, they stand for a raw, authentic riding style where the focus is entirely on the riding experience. Yamaha's MT series - including models such as the MT-07 or MT-09 - is particularly popular and is known for its powerful torque, agility in city traffic and sporty dynamics. Thanks to their low weight and upright riding position, naked bikes offer a direct, unadulterated riding experience and are suitable for both everyday use and extended tours. Anyone looking for a motorbike that combines performance, style and suitability for everyday use will find Yamaha's naked bikes a convincing choice.
